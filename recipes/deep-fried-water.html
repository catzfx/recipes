Deep Fried Water

This recipe is another staple of the American kitchen, although it is most commonly used by peeps going on diets, because of the low caloric content.

Feel free to follow allong with the video below, or read along like an old-timer.

Ingredients:

  • Sodium alginate
  • Calcium chloride
  • Water
  • Vegetable oil
  • Flour of any kind
  • Eggs
  • Breadcrumbs

This recipe is pretty flexible. Feel free to choose your amounts.

Steps:

  1. In a bowl, mix up water and sodium alginate.
  2. In another bowl, mix up water and calcium chloride.
  3. Take a few spoons of the first bowl's contents, and carefully insert them into the second bowl.
  4. While we're waiting for the solution to solidify, prepare plates with flour, scrambled egg whites and yolks, and breadcrumbs, and put a pan on medium heat, pouring the vegetable oil in it.
  5. Carefully take out the water 'bubbles' formed in the bowl from step 3 with a soft instrument (ideally your hand), then soak them in the flour, eggs, and breadcrumbs, then drop them in the pan.
  6. After a few seconds, take them out.

You're done!

Enjoy your tasty, healthy, American-style diet food!
